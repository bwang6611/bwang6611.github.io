Jakob, Just in a Slump or Fallen Off Forever?

Jakob, a revered high elo part of the Valorant squad has been preforming very poorly in the past games, is he just not focused or has his age caught up with him?

Jakob has been a dominant force on the Valorant team for quite a while now, in previous years he has paired up with BTV, Abe and Ernie and has been seemingly unstoppable. However recently, it is plain to see Jakob has not been performing to the standards the fans have come to expect from him. Is this just a bit of a temporary period or could Jakob have lost his edge for the rest of his career? Follow this page for more updates.
